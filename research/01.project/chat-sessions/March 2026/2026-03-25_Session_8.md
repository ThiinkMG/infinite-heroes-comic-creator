## Prompt Engineering Notes

The AI now receives emblem and clothing reinforcement at multiple layers:
1. **Layer 1** (Reference Images): Labels include placement (e.g., "HERO EMBLEM/LOGO (MUST appear on chest center)")
2. **Layer 2** (Identity Headers): Includes emblem with placement and clothing details
3. **Layer 4** (Consistency Instructions): Explicit per-character emblem and clothing requirements
4. **Critical Directives**: Global enforcement rules (directives #5 and #6)

This multi-layer approach ensures the AI receives consistent instructions throughout the prompt, reducing drift and improving character consistency.
